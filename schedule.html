2018 Session Schedule

The core program runs for 11 weeks (June 26th - September 9th). The typical summer schedule consists of: up to 2 personalized tutoring sessions per week (about 30 minutes each); 5 practice sessions per week (1 hour each); and daily brain training (10 minutes each).


Students take the first official SAT exam of the fall on August 26th. They then take the first official ACT exam of the fall (September 9th). Students have the option to continue with the program and take additional ACT and SAT exams.


It is expected that students will take a 1 week vacation during the summer during which no studying will be required. InspireDo is able to create custom schedules to accommodate each student’s needs.
