Program Cost Details

The charge for the InspireDo session will be based on whichever test (ACT or SAT) yields the greatest score improvement. The session charge is due after official test scores have been received and the score improvement can be determined.


Each student’s baseline ACT score will be their Composite Score from taking an official ACT practice test at the beginning of the session. The Composite Score is the average of the 4 core ACT sections (Math, English, Reading and Science).


Each student’s baseline SAT score will be determined by using the official ACT to SAT concordance table.
