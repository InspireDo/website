Innovative Approach

InspireDo has developed an entirely new approach for preparing for the SAT/ACT, an approach that has generated incredible results. We incorporate the latest in brain training and motivational research coming out of Stanford, Harvard, Columbia, Princeton and other top universities. Our students prepare for the exams much as world class athletes prepare for the Olympics. Some of the key strategies we focus on to help our students optimize both their training and test day performance are:

  • Breath control
  • Focus endurance
  • Performance visualization
  • Task prioritization
  • Self-image enrichment
  • Motivation enhancement

Advanced Technology

Technology is an essential component of the InspireDo program. We leverage and have created some of the most progressive technologies available today:

  • The InspireDo application has been custom built to help motivate students. It utilizes powerful technology from Google and couples it with the latest research on motivation, focus and effort.

  • Gamification components bring a fun, competitive aspect to the training. Daily and weekly competitions make the study process more exciting and motivating.

  • Khan Academy is helping to change the world with its online learning platform. We leverage this platform along with other proven training content to give the students the information they need for success.

Flexible Scheduling

The key to success is consistent, focused practice. An average of 1 hour per day is what we ask our students to commit to studying during the summer.

A unique weekly schedule is created for each student taking into account their other commitments. Each student is expected to take a 1 week vacation where no studying is required.
